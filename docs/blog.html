Open Source Business

The pitfalls and proven solutions for open source businesses.

Some developers want to create projects that are fully transparent to the public, open for all to see and spread. However, many of these projects fails to take off or struggle to maintain their presence due to the lack of financial support from the community. In theory, if their product is good, people will donate; but--more often than not--these projects sink due to a lack of donations.
